Degree in Mathematics


This major is designed to offer you quality education in mathematics by providing you with a strong background in the skills of logic, reasoning, critical thinking, and technology. It covers the traditional aspect of any mathematics major and offers courses that cover contemporary topics in mathematics. It also offers a combination of pure and applied mathematics courses, thus exposing you to mathematical fields that are useful in a multitude of applications such as the physical sciences and engineering, among others




Career


This major prepares you for a wide range of careers such as teaching,Cryptographer,Market Analyst or Researcher.It also prepares students to pursue graduate studies in mathematics, applied mathematics, or other related fields.






Curriculum


A minimum of (180 credits) are required,and can be completed in three years in order to complete the Bachelor's Degree, and (60 credits) in master 1 and master 2, each year consist of two semesters,and each semester consist of (30 credit),in master 2 the student can choose one of our majors that are listed below in order to continue his PHD later.
The required areas of study include core mathematics courses to form solid foundation in the areas of real analysis,complex analysis,linear algebra,abstract algebra,probability,and statistics.
